The Investment Game (Brief)

You have the opportunity to buy-in to this game next week with $30,000. Your job is to analyze the potential outcomes of the game and communicate why you should or should not buy-in.

Each year after buy-in you flip a fair coin:

  • Heads: increase your account balance by 50%
  • Tails: decrease your account balance by 40%

You play annually until age 75. Your mission is to analyze outcomes and communicate insights clearly.

6) The Kelly Criterion

The Kelly Criterion is a mathematical formula used to determine how much of your capital to risk on a bet or investment in order to maximize long-term growth. By calculating the original game, the Kelly Criterion would not suggest palying this game because the result is −0.5. Only use 25% of the balance to gamble each time, the Kelly Criterion would suggest playing this game because the result is 0.65%, it means you balance will slowly increase over time. It is much safer than the original game.
